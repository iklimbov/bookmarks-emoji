bookmarks + emoji

features + faq 

Up to 512 icons can be replaced with emojis.

Emoji is assigned to domain + path combination. That means, assigning emoji to one bookmark will assign the same emoji to bookmarks with similar URL.

Not all emojis are supported by all operating systems. If your emoji is not displayed properly (usually you will see a grey rectangle) simply replace emoji.

Please report bugs and suggest new features using "contact" form. I will also be glad to receive your collaboration requests and ideas for new projects.

If you like my work, post a review and share it with your friends.

Customizing Bookmarks with Emoji

Replace default icons for bookmarks or folders with emoji. Right click icon to customize and select Emoji dialog. Alternatively, this option is available in the Edit dialog. Take your time selecting meaningful emojis. Use "Remove emoji" button to remove currently selected emoji.

Simple Tree

In this view bookmarks are organized into the tree with titles beside icons. Sort bookmarks by titles or number of uses.

Icons only

But what if you know your bookmarks pretty well? Do you really want to read titles every time? In icons-only view, sorting is not super intuitive.

Tree vs Single Folder View

This button lets you select between tree and single-folder views.

Tree view is like a traditional file explorer view on your computer.Folder View includes navigator panel with shortcuts to all available folders. The content of the selected folder is presented below the breadcrumbs.To make the most out of single folder navigator, take your time and set custom emoji icons for all your folders. You will not regret the result!

Archiving

Bookmarks not used during past 90 days are moved to the Archive folder, located under the Other Bookmarks. Review them and get rid of them, if you wish.

Deleting

Deleted bookmarks are moved into Recycling Bin. Deleting from Recycling Bin cannot be undone. Recycling Bin is located under Other Bookmarks.

More...

Switch the lights off to use dark mode. Use search to quickly find bookmarks and folders.
